Strategy:

From vision and strategy to execution and success. A sustainable strategy is devised to achieve the maximum, a need-based and right-based approach initiated ascertains the problem with full community involvement and mobilizing all indigenous and local resources. The program goal is materialized with a helping hand from members donor agencies, state and federal institutions, philanthropists, and diaspora communities.

While implementing its projects as a first step undertakes community mobilization so that to raise awareness in the community on health and hygiene, the importance of education in modern-day affairs, gender-related issues, socio-economic development and environmental protection. For this purpose, TADO Org. management holds meetings with the community members, conducts training/capacity building sessions and launches awareness campaigns.

TADO Org. also believes in advocacy and therefore, the management has always strongly advocated for the issues related to TADO Org. For achieving this objective, TADO Org. through its strong advocacy has approached the line departments of the government of Somalia and state administrations, NGOs, CBOs, civil society organizations (CSOs), members of the community, notables and TADO Org. has always promoted the right-based approach and supported the dialogue as an important tool for achieving the above-mentioned objectives.

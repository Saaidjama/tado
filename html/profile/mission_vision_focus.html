Mission Statment & Vision

Mission Statment:

The mission of TADO organization is to contribute to the health and education of young people, through a value system based, and to achieve human rights, sustainable humanitarian services, and equitable socio-economic development that promote peace and justice for all.

Vision:

We envision our organization enabling all people in the community to live with dignity, freedom, peace, and prosperity.

Areas of Focus:

  1. Education and Skill Development
  2. Health, Nutrition & WASH
  3. Youth and Women Empowerment
  4. Community Physical Infrastructures
  5. Livelihood Improvement
  6. Emergency Response and Preparedness
  7. Awareness towards Peace and Reconciliation
